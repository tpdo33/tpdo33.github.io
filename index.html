Why hello there

Electrical Engineering Student | GIS

My name is Theresa and I am an electrical engineering student, musician, chef, gamer, traveler, GIS specialist, and a delightful misfit. One of the many passions I have in life is music. My love for traveling guided me to study and major in GIS. After graduating from the University of Washington, I was given the opportunity to work for Google Maps as a contractor. During my time there, my curiosity for technology advanced from watching an idea transform from the whiteboard into something that millions of people use everyday. My eureka moment hit when I realized I could relate all of my passions in life to technology. This finally urged me to research into ways to expand my skills set and I enrolled into a coding bootcamp. Since graduating I have chosen to take a step further in my education and pursue my engineering degree in Electrical Engineering because what’s stopping me from learning more?
